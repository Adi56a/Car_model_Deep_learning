Pioneering Traffic Intelligence

Final Year Project

RoadSense AI represents the culmination of cutting-edge research in computer vision and artificial intelligence. Our system transforms ordinary traffic cameras into intelligent sensors that can identify lane markings, detect objects.

Developed as a final year project, this technology demonstrates how affordable cameras can replace expensive sensors, making advanced safety systems accessible to all communities and transportation authorities.

  • Real-time processing at 60+ frames per second
  • Works in various lighting and weather conditions
  • Integration with existing traffic infrastructure
  • Open-source architecture for continuous improvement

Lane Detection Technology

Stay in the lines, automatically

Our advanced lane detection system uses convolutional neural networks to identify lane markings even in challenging conditions. The algorithm processes video feeds frame-by-frame to track road boundaries with exceptional accuracy.

  • Real-time lane boundary identification
  • Works on unmarked or faded roads
  • Night vision capability
  • Curve prediction for proactive steering assistance

Object Detection System

See what matters, instantly

Our object detection algorithm can identify and classify multiple objects simultaneously, from pedestrians and cyclists to vehicles and road signs. With millisecond processing times, the system provides drivers with crucial information before human perception can react.

  • Multi-class object recognition
  • Distance and trajectory estimation
  • Collision risk assessment
  • 99.7% accuracy in standard conditions
